TEDX PESU
In my first year at PES University, I was a member of TEDxPESU, which is an attempt to bring the TED format to PESU and provide for deep and engaging talks. It serves as a platform for the PESU community to share, discuss and go beyond ideas, with annual events and smaller activites such as TED circles and TED Salons. I was a member of the Design domain and worked on various branding deliverables for the promotion of the events.
