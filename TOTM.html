Tomb Of The Mask: Game Re-Skinning
In my recent project for my Advanced Game Design class, I led the efforts to create a new skin for an existing hyper-casual game called ‘Tomb of the Mask’. The primary goals included developing a cohesive visual language and understanding core principles such as player retention and LTV, and the addition of meta features like Daily Puzzles.  
Throughout the process, we implemented vibrant visuals and user-friendly design elements, that still kept the functionality of the original game. This experience taught me the importance of balancing creativity with designs under themes (in this case, Egyptian Flat Art) and constraints, influencing my approach in subsequent projects.
