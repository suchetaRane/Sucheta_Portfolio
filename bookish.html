Bookish: Study Space Booking App
This project designed a simple UI for a mobile study space booking app. My teammate and I started by researching student needs and understanding the issue of overcrowding in study spaces.
After thorough research and observational studies, multiple iterations, and through faculty and peer feedback, we created a platform where students could book slots for study purposes based on their requirements and preferences.
