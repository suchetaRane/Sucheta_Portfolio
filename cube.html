Graphic Design
Destination: Istanbul

In a graphic design class, we were assigned with designing a cube with all six sides being cohesive and following the theme, “Ultimate Travel Destination”. My choice was Istanbul, Turkey. After copious amounts of research, I came up with these six frames that all connect to eachother, and represent various cultural aspects of Istanbul.

The geometric type and the Tombili cat ones are my personal favourites.
