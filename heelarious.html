Branding
Heelarious

For a branding class, I was tasked with creating a brand identity based on personal interests.

I created “Heelarious”, a sock brand known for its whimsical, silly and funny sock designs.

 

The primary objective of this project was to learn branding principles and strategies. This experience taught me what makes brands unique, recognisable and reputational. The pre-launch posts that are displayed above can also be found on the Instagram page:
